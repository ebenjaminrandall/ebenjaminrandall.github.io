About

Over 20 years of experience

I have tutored students at every level from middle school through graduate school, with a primary focus on mathematics and sciences. I tailor each session to the student, whether that means building intuition from scratch, filling in gaps, or preparing for a high-stakes exam.

Sessions are available virtually via Zoom. I do my best to accommodate schedules, with regular availability on Sunday, Monday, and Wednesday evenings. Contact me to discuss your needs and rates.
